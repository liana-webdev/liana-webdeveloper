About me

My name is Liana. You probably have already guessed that I am a web developer. I'm into design too.

A little bit about my journey

I started to learn web development 3 years ago because I was curious about it, never thought this was going to become my career. I am not going to tell you that I studied really hard everyday to get to where I am now, because I didn't do it. I learned at my own pace just for fun, then I realized that I can actually sell my work.

Well, after 1 year I was seriously thinking to make web development my job, but things were so hard for me back then. So, I started to do more work in 2019. I learned the basics of web design and viola, I got my first client. I was so happy and surprised because I knew that I didn't have working for someone experience at all, and this is exactly when I started to push myself. It took about 3-5 weeks to complete including that I was learning on the go, which is mindblowing. Mingblowing because I accepted the challenge to build a website that I wasn't sure I can build, but I did it, my client was happy and I got paid my first $150 (which I thought was a lot for a multipage, responsive website with plenty of images, lol). You can find this project below by the way.

I broke so many boundaries and even still I do the same when I learn something new. I am always learning. It keeps me inspired for more professional work. I am also very creative, making things look pretty and creating smooth animations is my favourite.
